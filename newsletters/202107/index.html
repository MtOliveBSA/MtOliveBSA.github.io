Marauder Mayhem 2021

Come join us July 23rd 5-9pm at Turkey Brook Park for MOBSA Marauder Mayhem 2021! Players can compete in a series of events for Pitch, Hit & Run as well as a Home Run Derby. Skull Rock Concessions will be open and there will be music and game, so come compete or come to join the fun!
